Dessert Recipies

Vanilia Cheesecake

  • 100g sugar
  • 450g cream cheese (Philadelphia)
  • 600g double cream
  • 1 vanilla pod (scraped)
  • 1 lemon zest

Place the ingredients in a machine bowl and whisk together until firm. Layer the mixture with crushed digestive biscuits mixed with melted butter and store in small plastic containers.

Chocolate Brownie

  • 1kg butter cut into small pieces
  • 1.4kg dark chocolate buttons
  • 1 kg whole egg
  • 1.5kg sugar
  • 250g strong flour
  • 25g baking powder
  • 250g cocoa powder
  • 600g pecan nuts crushed

Pre heat oven to 175Âºc Melt the chocolate and butter over a bain marie. Whisk the sugar and eggs to make a sabayon that has doubled in size. Fold the 2 mixes together, sieve the dry ingredients together and fold under the chocolate mix. Fold in the nuts and place in silicone lined baking trays. Bake for 15 minutes and cool on oven racks before chilling in the fridge. Portion the brownie to 2cm by 6cm rectangles and store in air tight containers.
